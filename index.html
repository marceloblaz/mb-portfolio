About me

In my 8 years of experience in Product Design I’ve worked across multiple contexts—financial institutions, agencies, and startups—always in close collaboration with cross-functional teams. I thrive in ambiguous environments by breaking down complexity and creating clarity around products.

At Galley, I helped shape Menu Planner, a core module that enabled customers to cut planning time in half, freeing them to focus on crafting better menus.

After experiencing the pain of setting proper line length in Figma, I built a plugin to solve it—reducing a typical 1½-minute workflow to just 4 seconds. The plugin is now used by over 6,000 designers.

I help teams build more intuitive products by shaping design systems, workflows, and user experiences. Let’s talk.

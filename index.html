About me

I'm Marcelo Brasileiro, a product designer based in Brasília, Brazil, with over six years of experience designing tools, platforms, and systems for companies around the world.

I specialize in connecting high-level thinking with interface craft. My approach is generalist by design — I enjoy working across research, UX, UI, and implementation, especially in complex problem spaces. Whether helping a startup validate a new idea or refining an internal platform at scale, I focus on clarity, adaptability, and thoughtful design.

I'm also a certified Object-Oriented UX strategist, which means I bring strong systems thinking into my process. Outside of work, I'm usually learning something new, organizing my workshop, or tweaking a Figma plugin I built that's used by thousands of designers.

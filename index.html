About me

I’m Marcelo Brasileiro, a product designer based in Brasília, Brazil, with over six years of experience designing tools, platforms, and systems for companies around the world.

I specialize in bridging high-level product thinking with practical execution. My generalist approach allows me to work across strategy, research, and implementation — especially in complex or ambiguous problem spaces. Whether it’s helping a startup shape a new product or refining enterprise software at scale, I focus on clarity, adaptability, and delivering real value.
